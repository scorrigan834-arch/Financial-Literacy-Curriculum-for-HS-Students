# Vision

Every high school graduate should possess the financial knowledge, skills, and confidence necessary to make informed decisions about money, education, debt, investing, and wealth building.

## Our Mission

Our mission is to create a free, collaborative, and practical financial literacy curriculum that prepares students for the financial decisions they will face throughout life.

## Long-Term Goals

- Improve financial literacy among high school students
- Reduce costly financial mistakes
- Increase understanding of investing and wealth building
- Help students evaluate college and career decisions
- Create a curriculum that can be adopted by schools nationwide

## Guiding Principles

- Practical over theoretical
- Real-world application
- Accessible to all students
- Continuously improved through community contributions
