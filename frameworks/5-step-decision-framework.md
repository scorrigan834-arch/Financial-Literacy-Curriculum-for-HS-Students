# The Future Ready Decision Framework

## Before making any major financial decision, ask five questions.

### 1. What is the total cost?

Not just today.

What is the full cost over time?

---

### 2. What are the alternatives?

Most financial mistakes happen when people only consider one option.

---

### 3. What is the opportunity cost?

If you spend money here, what are you giving up elsewhere?

---

### 4. What are the long-term consequences?

How could this decision affect your future self?

---

### 5. Does this move me closer to or further from my goals?

The best financial decision is often the one that aligns with your long-term objectives.

---

## Remember

The goal is not to make perfect decisions.

The goal is to make informed decisions.
