# Contributing

Thank you for your interest in improving financial literacy for students.

We welcome contributions from educators, financial professionals, students, parents, and industry experts.

---

## Ways to Contribute

### Curriculum Development

Help create or improve:

* Lessons
* Activities
* Simulations
* Assessments
* Worksheets

---

### Classroom Testing

Pilot individual lessons or modules and provide feedback.

---

### Content Review

Review existing materials for:

* Accuracy
* Clarity
* Relevance
* Student engagement

---

### Industry Expertise

Share real-world insights related to:

* Banking
* Investing
* Credit
* Taxes
* Career readiness
* Wealth building

---

## Contribution Process

1. Review the Curriculum Map.
2. Identify an area where you can contribute.
3. Open an Issue describing your suggestion.
4. Submit proposed changes through a Pull Request.

---

## Guiding Principles

Contributions should be:

* Practical
* Student-centered
* Nonpartisan
* Easy for educators to implement
* Focused on real-world decision making

---

## Our Goal

To help every student graduate with the knowledge and confidence to make informed financial decisions.
