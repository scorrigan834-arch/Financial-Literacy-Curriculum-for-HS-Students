# Should I Go To College?

## One of the biggest financial decisions you may ever make.

The goal is not to answer this question for you.

The goal is to help you evaluate the decision.

---

## Questions to Consider

### What career are you pursuing?

### Is a degree required?

### What is the expected salary?

### What is the total cost?

### How much debt would you need?

### What alternatives exist?

---

## Potential Benefits

* Increased earnings
* Career opportunities
* Professional network

---

## Potential Risks

* Student loan debt
* Opportunity cost
* Degree-job mismatch

---

## Financial Exercise

Calculate:

* Total education cost
* Expected debt
* Expected starting salary
* Estimated repayment timeline

---

## Final Reflection

> If I choose college, what am I hoping this investment produces?
