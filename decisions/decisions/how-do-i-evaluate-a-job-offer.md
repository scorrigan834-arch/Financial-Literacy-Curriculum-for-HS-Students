# How Do I Evaluate a Job Offer?

## A job offer is more than a salary.

Many people focus on one number.

The best decisions evaluate the complete opportunity.

---

## Areas to Evaluate

### Compensation

* Base salary
* Bonus opportunities
* Commission structure

---

### Benefits

* Health insurance
* Retirement plans
* Employer match
* Paid time off

---

### Growth Opportunities

* Training
* Mentorship
* Advancement potential

---

### Lifestyle

* Commute
* Remote flexibility
* Work-life balance

---

### Stability

* Company health
* Industry outlook
* Career path

---

## Questions to Ask

### What will my take-home pay actually be?

### What opportunities does this create?

### What risks exist?

### Will this position help me reach my long-term goals?

---

## Reality Check

The highest salary is not always the best opportunity.

The best opportunity is the one that aligns with your goals.

---

## Final Reflection

> Five years from now, will I be glad I accepted this opportunity?
