# Am I On Track Financially?

## A question nearly everyone asks.

The challenge is that many people compare themselves to others instead of evaluating their own progress.

Financial success looks different for everyone.

The goal is not perfection.

The goal is progress.

---

## Areas to Review

### Income

Is your income growing over time?

---

### Savings

Are you consistently setting money aside?

---

### Debt

Is debt decreasing or becoming more manageable?

---

### Investing

Are you building long-term assets?

---

### Emergency Preparedness

Could you handle an unexpected expense?

---

### Goals

Do your financial decisions support your future plans?

---

## Questions to Ask

### Am I making progress?

### Am I learning from past mistakes?

### Am I becoming more financially resilient?

### Am I building options for my future?

---

## Reality Check

Most people feel behind.

Progress is usually more important than comparison.

---

## Final Reflection

> If I continue my current habits for the next five years, where am I likely to end up?
