# How Much Student Debt Is Too Much?

## One of the most important financial questions you'll ever answer.

Student loans can help open doors.

They can also limit future choices if you borrow more than your future income can reasonably support.

The goal is not to avoid all debt.

The goal is to understand the consequences before borrowing.

---

## The Wrong Question

Many students ask:

> Can I get approved for this loan?

A better question is:

> Can I comfortably repay this loan?

---

## Questions to Ask

### What is the total amount I will borrow?

### What will my monthly payment be?

### What is my expected starting salary?

### How stable is my career field?

### What alternatives exist?

* Community college
* Trade school
* Scholarships
* Working while attending school

---

## Red Flags

⚠️ Borrowing more than your expected first-year salary.

⚠️ Borrowing without understanding monthly payments.

⚠️ Choosing a school based only on prestige.

⚠️ Assuming future income will solve today's borrowing decisions.

---

## Reality Check

Every dollar borrowed today is future income already committed.

Before borrowing, ask:

> Will my future self be thankful for this decision?

---

## Final Reflection

The goal is not simply to attend college.

The goal is to create opportunities without limiting your future flexibility.
