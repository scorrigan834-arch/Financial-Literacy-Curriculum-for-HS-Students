# New Car vs. Used Car

## One of the most common financial decisions young adults make.

A vehicle can provide freedom and convenience.

It can also become one of the largest expenses in your budget.

The goal is not to determine which option is always better.

The goal is to understand the tradeoffs.

---

## New Car Advantages

* Warranty coverage
* Latest safety features
* Lower maintenance risk
* Greater reliability

---

## New Car Disadvantages

* Higher purchase price
* Higher insurance costs
* Rapid depreciation
* Larger monthly payments

---

## Used Car Advantages

* Lower purchase price
* Slower depreciation
* Lower insurance costs
* Reduced financing needs

---

## Used Car Disadvantages

* Potential maintenance costs
* Limited warranty protection
* Older technology

---

## Questions to Ask

### What is the total cost?

### How much will insurance cost?

### How long do I plan to keep the vehicle?

### How will this affect my savings goals?

---

## Reality Check

Many people focus on the monthly payment.

The smarter approach is to evaluate the total cost of ownership.

---

## Final Reflection

> Is this vehicle helping me build my future or delaying my financial goals?
