# Should I Start Investing Now?

## The most valuable resource in investing is time.

Many people delay investing because they believe they need:

* More money
* More knowledge
* Better timing

In reality, starting early is often more important than starting perfectly.

---

## Why People Delay

### "I don't make enough money."

### "I still have debt."

### "I don't understand investing."

### "I'll start later."

---

## Questions to Consider

### Do I have an emergency fund?

### Do I understand the risks?

### Am I investing for the long term?

### What other financial priorities exist?

---

## The Cost of Waiting

Every year delayed is a year that money is not working on your behalf.

Time can be one of the most powerful factors in long-term wealth building.

---

## Reality Check

Investing is not about getting rich quickly.

It is about consistently participating in long-term growth.

---

## Final Reflection

> If my future self could invest one additional year earlier, would they?
