# Can I Afford This Apartment?

## Rent is more than the monthly payment.

Many people focus on rent and overlook the full cost of living independently.

Before signing a lease, understand the complete financial picture.

## Questions to Ask

### Can I still save money every month?

### Can I afford unexpected expenses?

### Would I be financially stressed if my income changed?

### How much flexibility would I have left?

---

## Warning Signs

⚠️ Living paycheck to paycheck.

⚠️ Using credit cards to cover basic expenses.

⚠️ Having no emergency fund.

⚠️ Spending so much on housing that other goals become impossible.

---

## Reality Check

The goal is not to qualify for an apartment.

The goal is to afford the life that comes with it.

---

## Final Reflection

Ask yourself:

> Will this apartment improve my quality of life, or simply consume more of my income?
