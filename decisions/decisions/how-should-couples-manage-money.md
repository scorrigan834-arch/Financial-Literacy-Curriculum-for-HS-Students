# How Should Couples Manage Money?

## One of the most important conversations many couples never have.

Financial stress often comes from expectations that were never discussed.

There is no single correct system.

The best system is the one both people understand and support.

---

## Common Approaches

### Separate Accounts

Each person manages their own finances.

---

### Joint Accounts

Income and expenses are combined.

---

### Hybrid Systems

A combination of shared and individual accounts.

---

## Questions to Discuss

### What are our financial goals?

### How will we handle major purchases?

### How will we manage debt?

### What role should saving and investing play?

### How will we communicate about money?

---

## Warning Signs

⚠️ Avoiding financial conversations.

⚠️ Hiding spending.

⚠️ Making major financial decisions independently.

⚠️ Assuming both people share the same priorities.

---

## Reality Check

Financial success in a relationship often depends more on communication than math.

---

## Final Reflection

> Are we building a financial plan together or simply sharing expenses?
