# Financial Literacy Curriculum

## Executive Summary

### Mission

Help students understand the financial consequences of life's biggest decisions before they make them.

---

## The Problem

Many students graduate high school without understanding:

- Student loans
- Credit and debt
- Budgeting
- Investing
- Taxes
- Housing decisions

As a result, young adults often face major financial decisions without the tools needed to evaluate long-term consequences.

---

## Our Solution

The Financial Literacy Curriculum is an open-source, decision-based learning program designed for high school students.

Instead of focusing solely on financial concepts, students learn through:

- Simulations
- Real-world scenarios
- Long-term consequence modeling
- Decision-making exercises

---

## What Makes This Different

Traditional programs often teach:

- Definitions
- Formulas
- Terminology

This curriculum teaches:

- Decision-making
- Tradeoff analysis
- Long-term thinking
- Real-world application

Students learn how choices related to education, careers, debt, housing, and investing affect future outcomes.

---

## Curriculum Topics

1. Money Mindset
2. Budgeting & Cash Flow
3. Banking
4. Credit & Debt
5. College & Student Loans
6. Career & Income
7. Investing
8. Taxes
9. Housing & Major Purchases
10. Insurance & Risk
11. Wealth Building

---

## Signature Experience

### Design Your Financial Future

Students build a model of their future by making decisions regarding:

- Education
- Career
- Housing
- Debt
- Saving
- Investing

and evaluate the long-term consequences of those choices.

---

## Long-Term Vision

Every student should graduate with the knowledge, confidence, and decision-making skills needed to build a financially successful future.
