# Future Ready Principles

## The ideas behind every lesson, simulation, and decision guide.

---

## Principle 1

### Understand consequences before making decisions.

Financial decisions often create outcomes that last for years.

---

## Principle 2

### Time is your greatest financial asset.

Starting early often matters more than starting perfectly.

---

## Principle 3

### Income helps. Systems matter more.

A good financial system can outperform a high income with poor habits.

---

## Principle 4

### Every dollar has a purpose.

Intentional spending creates flexibility.

Unintentional spending creates stress.

---

## Principle 5

### Avoid decisions you do not understand.

If you cannot explain the decision, continue learning before acting.

---

## Principle 6

### Debt should create opportunities, not limitations.

Understand the tradeoffs before borrowing.

---

## Principle 7

### Wealth is built through consistency.

Financial success is rarely the result of one perfect decision.

It is the result of thousands of small decisions.

---

## Principle 8

### Compare yourself to your past self, not other people.

Progress matters more than comparison.

---

## Principle 9

### Financial confidence comes from understanding.

Knowledge reduces uncertainty.

---

## Principle 10

### Build the life you want, not the one others expect.

Money is a tool.

The goal is to support the life you want to create.
