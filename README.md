# Financial Literacy Curriculum

## Mission

Our mission is to ensure every high school student graduates with the financial knowledge and decision-making skills necessary to navigate adulthood confidently.

## Why This Project Exists

Millions of students graduate high school with limited exposure to:

- Budgeting
- Banking
- Credit
- Student Loans
- Investing
- Taxes
- Insurance
- Wealth Building

This open-source curriculum aims to change that.

## Who Should Contribute?

We welcome contributions from:

- Teachers
- School administrators
- Financial advisors
- College financial aid professionals
- Parents
- Students
- Researchers

## Curriculum Roadmap

### Foundations

- Money Mindset
- Goal Setting
- Budgeting

### Banking & Credit

- Checking Accounts
- Savings Accounts
- Credit Scores
- Credit Cards

### Major Financial Decisions

- College Financing
- Student Loans
- Career ROI
- Housing

### Wealth Building

- Investing
- Retirement
- Taxes
- Entrepreneurship
