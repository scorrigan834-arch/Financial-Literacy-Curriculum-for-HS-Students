# Financial Decisions

## Life is a series of financial decisions.

Every major financial outcome begins with a decision.

Future Ready Finance helps you understand the consequences before those decisions become real.

---

* Should I go to college?
* Should I attend community college first?
* Is graduate school worth it?
* How much student debt is too much?

## Career

* How do I evaluate a job offer?

* When should I ask for a raise?

## Housing

* Should I rent or buy?

* Should I live with roommates?

---

## Transportation

* Should I buy or lease?

* What can I actually afford?

## Investing

* When should I start?
