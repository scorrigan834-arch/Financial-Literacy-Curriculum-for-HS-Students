# You Probably Can't Afford That

## A reality check before making a major purchase.

Many people ask:

> Can I make the payment?

A better question is:

> Can I afford everything that comes with it?

---

## Consider:

* Insurance
* Maintenance
* Repairs
* Opportunity cost
* Lost investing potential
* Reduced flexibility

---

## Ask Yourself

### Can I still save?

### Can I still invest?

### Can I handle an emergency?

### Will this purchase limit future opportunities?

---

## Reality Check

Affording the payment is not the same thing as affording the purchase.

---

> Financial freedom often comes from the things you choose not to buy.
