# Can I Actually Afford This?

## A simple framework for evaluating purchases.

Before buying anything significant, ask:

### Can I pay for it without creating financial stress?

### Will I still be happy with this purchase six months from now?

### What else could this money accomplish?

### Am I buying this because I need it or because I want it?

### Is this decision aligned with my goals?

---

## Reality Check

Most financial problems are not caused by one large mistake.

They are caused by hundreds of small decisions made without a process.

Use a process.
