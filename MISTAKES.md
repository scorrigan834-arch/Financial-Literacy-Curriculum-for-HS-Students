# Financial Mistakes

## Learn from mistakes before they become expensive.

Most financial stress is not caused by one catastrophic decision.

It is often caused by small mistakes repeated over time.

Future Ready Finance helps you recognize common mistakes before they affect your future.

---

## Early Mistakes

* Ignoring student loan repayment
* Carrying credit card balances
* Living paycheck to paycheck
* Delaying investing

---

## Career Mistakes

* Focusing only on salary
* Ignoring benefits
* Not negotiating compensation
* Lifestyle inflation

---

## Housing Mistakes

* Buying too much house
* Renting without a budget
* Underestimating maintenance costs

---

## Investing Mistakes

* Waiting for the perfect time
* Following trends
* Taking risks you don't understand

---

## Family & Wealth Mistakes

* Avoiding financial conversations
* Failing to plan
* Not protecting income and assets

---

> Every mistake can become a lesson if you learn from it early enough.
