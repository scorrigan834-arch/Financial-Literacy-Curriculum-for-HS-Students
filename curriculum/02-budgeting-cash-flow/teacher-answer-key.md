## Suggested Discussion Points

### Needs vs Wants

Students should understand:

* Needs support basic living
* Wants improve quality of life

Some categories may contain elements of both.

---

### Emergency Funds

Key takeaway:

Unexpected expenses are inevitable.

Emergency savings reduce reliance on debt.

---

### Lifestyle Inflation

Students should recognize:

* Higher income does not automatically create wealth
* Savings rate matters more than income alone

## Module Goal

Students should understand:

1. Cash flow drives financial stability
