# First Apartment Budget Challenge

## Objective

Students will create and manage a realistic monthly budget while balancing housing, transportation, food, savings, and personal spending.

---

## Scenario

You are 22 years old and have just accepted your first full-time job.

You must create a monthly budget that allows you to:

* Pay your bills
* Save money
* Handle unexpected expenses
* Enjoy life responsibly
