# Lesson 4: Lifestyle Inflation

## Objective

Students will understand how increasing spending as income rises can prevent long-term wealth building.

---

## Essential Question

> Why do many people earn more money over time but still feel financially stressed?

---

## Learning Objectives

Students will be able to:

* Define lifestyle inflation
* Identify spending patterns that increase with income
* Explain how lifestyle inflation impacts savings
* Develop strategies to avoid lifestyle inflation

---

## Warm-Up Activity

Imagine you receive a raise that increases your take-home pay by $500 per month.

How would you use the extra money?

Write down your first reaction.

---

## Key Concepts

### What Is Lifestyle Inflation?

Lifestyle inflation occurs when spending increases as income increases.

Examples:

* Bigger apartment
* Newer car
* More dining out
* Additional subscriptions

---

### The Wealth Gap

Many people:

* Earn more
* Spend more
* Save the same

As a result, they never build significant wealth.

---

### Financial Flexibility

Increasing savings and investments alongside income creates:

* More freedom
* More opportunities
* Greater financial security

---

### The 50/50 Rule

One strategy:

When income increases:

* 50% toward improving lifestyle
* 50% toward saving and investing
