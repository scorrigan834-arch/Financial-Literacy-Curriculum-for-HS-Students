# Future You Simulation

## Objective

Students will compare two financial life paths and understand long-term consequences of financial behavior.

---

## Scenario

You are 18 years old.

You will simulate two versions of your financial future.

---

# Path A: Short-Term Spending Focus

* Spends most income
* Minimal savings
* Little or no investing
* Relies on credit when needed

---

# Path B: Long-Term Wealth Building

* Saves 15–25% of income
* Invests consistently
* Avoids high-interest debt
* Focuses on long-term planning

---

## Simulation Timeline

You will compare both paths at:

* Age 25
* Age 35
* Age 45

---

## Questions to Answer

1. What is the difference in net worth at each stage?

2. How does lifestyle differ between the two paths?

3. What tradeoffs exist in Path B early on?

4. Which path feels easier at age 18? Why?

---

## Reflection

Write a paragraph:

> Which version of your future self do you want to become, and what 1–2 habits would you need to change today?
