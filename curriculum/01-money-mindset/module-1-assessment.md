# Module 1 Assessment: Money Mindset

## Purpose

Assess student understanding of core financial mindset concepts.

---

## Section 1: Multiple Choice

1. Money is best described as:

* A. A goal
* B. A store of value and medium of exchange
* C. A government rule
* D. A personal opinion

2. Wealth is:

* A. Income
* B. Money earned monthly
* C. Net worth (assets minus liabilities)
* D. A salary

3. Delayed gratification means:

* A. Spending immediately
* B. Waiting for a larger future reward
* C. Avoiding all spending
* D. Borrowing money

---

## Section 2: Short Answer

1. Explain the difference between income and wealth.

2. Why can someone have a high income but low wealth?

3. How does behavior impact financial outcomes?

---

## Section 3: Real-World Scenario

A student earns $60,000 per year but spends almost all of it.

Another earns $45,000 but invests 20% consistently.

Which student is more likely to build wealth over time? Explain why.

---

## Section 4: Reflection

Write 5–7 sentences:

> What is the most important lesson from this module that will impact your future decisions?
