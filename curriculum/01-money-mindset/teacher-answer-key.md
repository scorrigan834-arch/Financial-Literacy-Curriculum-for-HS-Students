### Suggested Answer

Money is a medium of exchange, store of value, and unit of account.

Students should demonstrate understanding that money represents value and trust.

| Item               | Answer    |
| ------------------ | --------- |

| Student Loan       | Liability |
| Credit Card Debt   | Liability |
| Investment Account | Asset     |
| Auto Loan          | Liability |
| Cash               | Asset     |

---

### Net Worth Calculation

Assets:

* Cash = $5,000
* Investments = $12,000
* Vehicle = $8,000

Total Assets = $25,000

Liabilities:

* Student Loans = $10,000
* Credit Card Debt = $2,000

Total Liabilities = $12,000

Net Worth = $13,000

# Key Takeaways

Students should leave Module 1 understanding:

1. Money represents value and trust.
2. Wealth is different from income.
3. Financial behavior matters.
4. Small actions compound over time.
5. Long-term thinking creates better outcomes.
