# High School Path

## Build money confidence before graduation.

This path is designed for students preparing for first jobs, college decisions, trade school, student loans, budgeting, and financial independence.

## Key Questions

- Should I go to college, trade school, or start working?
- How much debt is too much?
- How do I build good money habits early?
- What does financial independence actually require?

---

## Recommended Outcome

By the end of this path, you should be able to explain the financial consequences of your first major adult decisions.
