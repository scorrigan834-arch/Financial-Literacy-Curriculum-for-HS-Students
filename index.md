## The Big Idea

Most people learn financial lessons after they make expensive mistakes.

**Future Ready Finance helps you learn them before the consequences become real.**

</div>

---

## What Makes This Different?

Most financial literacy courses teach students financial concepts.

**Future Ready Finance teaches students how to make financial decisions.**

Students learn through:

- Real-world simulations
- Long-term consequence modeling
- Student loan and career ROI analysis
- Budgeting challenges
- Capstone projects

---

## Core Modules

| Module | Focus |
|---|---|
| Money Mindset | Wealth, behavior, and long-term thinking |
| Budgeting & Cash Flow | Managing income, expenses, and stability |
| Banking | Checking, savings, interest, and fraud prevention |
| Credit & Debt | Borrowing, repayment, and credit decisions |
| College & Student Loans | Education financing and ROI |
| Investing | Compounding, risk, and long-term wealth |

## Goal

> Every student should graduate able to answer:
